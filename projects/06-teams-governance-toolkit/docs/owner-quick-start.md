# Team Owner Quick Start

## What Team Owners Are Accountable For

You are not expected to be a Teams administrator, but you are responsible for the workspace you sponsor.

Minimum owner responsibilities:

- keep at least two owners assigned
- remove members and guests who no longer need access
- confirm the team still serves a business purpose
- request archive or deletion when the work ends
- follow the approved naming and sensitivity rules

## Before You Create Or Request A Team

Check these first:

- Is there already a team serving the same purpose?
- Does the workspace need guests, or would a shared channel be enough?
- Will the team store sensitive HR, finance, or student data?
- Who is the backup owner if you leave the team?

## When To Use Guests

Use guest access when an external participant needs meaningful access to the team's files, channels, and conversations.

Do not use guests when:

- the collaboration is temporary and limited to a single workstream
- the team contains high-risk content not approved for external sharing
- there is no clear business sponsor

## When To Prefer Shared Channels

Prefer shared channels when:

- the external collaboration is limited to one partner lane
- you do not want the external party to see the entire team
- the collaboration is active but bounded

## Monthly Owner Checks

- confirm the second owner still exists and is active
- remove unused guests and external participants
- check whether the team still matches its stated purpose
- note any upcoming archive or renewal decision

## Red Flags

Escalate to admin or security support if:

- the team has only one owner
- the team is being used for regulated or highly sensitive data without a clear label
- guests are being added without a named sponsor
- a request asks to bypass naming, retention, or lifecycle rules permanently

## End Of Life

When the team is no longer needed:

1. remove guests if they no longer require access
2. confirm any retention or recordkeeping requirements
3. archive the team if business reference is still needed
4. request deletion only after archive and review if appropriate
