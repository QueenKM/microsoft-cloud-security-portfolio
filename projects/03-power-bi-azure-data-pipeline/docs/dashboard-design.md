# Dashboard Design

## Design Goal

Present one executive-friendly page that summarizes security pressure, governance drift, and operational maturity.

## Page Layout

## Top Row: KPI Cards

- total sign-in failures
- total incidents
- average compliance score
- active guest accounts
- net team growth
- privileged role changes

## Middle Row: Trend Visuals

- line chart for sign-in failures by month
- combo chart for incidents versus compliance score
- stacked column chart for incidents by workload

## Bottom Row: Governance Visuals

- bar chart for guest accounts by department
- clustered column chart for teams created versus archived
- matrix for department by workload with heat color on high incidents

## Slicers

- date
- department
- workload
- risk tier

## Storytelling Guidance

- use one accent color for security events and a separate accent color for governance activity
- show month-over-month trend, not only totals
- keep executive view focused on movement and ratios
- let the analyst drill into department and workload combinations
