# Business Questions

## Audience

This dataset is designed for two reporting audiences:

- executive or service-owner consumers who want fast KPI visibility
- security and governance analysts who need trend and workload breakdowns

## Core Questions

## 1. Security Pressure

- Which departments generate the highest total sign-in failures?
- Are high-severity incidents concentrated in one workload or spread across the environment?
- Is privileged role change activity stable or increasing?

## 2. Governance Drift

- Where are guest accounts increasing faster than lifecycle cleanup?
- Which departments create many teams but archive very few?
- Are collaboration controls lagging behind usage growth?

## 3. Hygiene And Maturity

- Is compliance score improving month over month?
- Are risky endpoints decreasing where incidents also decrease?
- Which workloads show the best ratio of incidents to compliance posture?

## Executive Storyline

The dashboard should help a stakeholder answer:

```text
Are we getting safer, or just busier?
```

## Analyst Storyline

The dashboard should help an analyst answer:

```text
Which department and workload combination needs attention first?
```
